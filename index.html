About Me

Over the last decade and beyond I seem to have added many strings to my bow. A journey that began working the red carpets at London awards shows, took me to Hollywood as a TV presenter and behind the cameras I established myself in business as Director and major shareholder of a successful marketing company.

On the surface it's been an incredible ride, experiencing many highs and of course plenty of lows along the way. But the inspiration that drives me to become better every day is the realisation only us as individuals can change and direct our chosen paths.

So everything on here is an expression and celebration of what positive energy can achieve. Through daily mindfulness and meditation exercises I've been able to build on my personal strengths and perhaps most importantly, develop upon my weaknesses to become a person I am truly happy to be. Someone who understands fulfilment comes through taking risks and embracing new opportunities, and now is the time to help you do the same. Welcome!
